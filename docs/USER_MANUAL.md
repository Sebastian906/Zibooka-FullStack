## 1. System Access

To access ZiBooka, open your web browser and go to the website URL.

```
https://zibooka-frontend.onrender.com/
```

[Homepage](images/english/screenshot_01.png)

### 1.1 New Account Registration

To create an account on ZiBooka, follow these steps:

1. Click on the user icon located in the upper right corner of the screen
2. Select the "Register" option
3. Complete the form with your information:
   - Full name
   - Email address
   - Password (minimum 8 characters)
4. Click "Create Account"

[Registration form](images/english/screenshot_02.png)

### 1.2 Login

If you already have an account:

1. Click on the user icon
2. Enter your email address and password
3. Click "Login"

[Login form](images/english/screenshot_03.png)

### 1.3 Password Recovery

If you forgot your password:

1. On the login screen, click "Forgot your password"
2. Enter the email address associated with your account
3. Check your inbox and follow the link sent
4. Enter your new password

[Password recovery screen](images/english/screenshot_04.png)

[Password change email](images/english/screenshot_05.png)

## 6. Book Loans

The loan system allows you to borrow books for a period of 14 days.

### 6.1 Requesting a Loan

1. Navigate to the book you want to borrow
2. Verify that the book is available
3. Click "Request Loan"
4. Confirm the request

[Request loan button](images/english/screenshot_15.png)

### 6.2 Viewing My Loans

To view your active loans:

1. Click on the user icon
2. Select "My Loans"
3. You will see the list of borrowed books with:
   - Book title
   - Loan date
   - Due date
   - Status (Active, Completed, Overdue)
   - Days remaining

[Loans list](images/english/screenshot_16.png)

### 6.3 Returning a Book

To return a book:

1. Access "My Loans"
2. Locate the book to return
3. Click "Return Book"
4. Confirm the return

The system will automatically update the book's availability.

### 6.4 Important Information

- Loans have a maximum duration of 14 days
- Overdue books may incur late fees
- Your most recent loans appear first in the list
- Return books on time to maintain a clean history

## 7. Reservations

If a book is not available, you can reserve it to be notified when it becomes available.

### 7.1 Reserving a Book

1. Navigate to the out-of-stock book
2. Click "Reserve"
3. You will be added to the waiting list

[Reserve out-of-stock book button](images/english/screenshot_17.png)

### 7.2 Viewing My Reservations

To view your reservations:

1. Click on the user icon
2. Select "My Reservations"
3. You will see the list of reservations with:
   - Book title
   - Request date
   - Position in waiting list
   - Reservation status
   - Expiration date

[Reservations list](images/english/screenshot_18.png)

### 7.3 Canceling a Reservation

If you no longer want to wait for a book:

1. Access "My Reservations"
2. Locate the reservation
3. Click "Cancel Reservation"
4. Confirm the cancellation

### 7.4 How the Waiting List Works

- Reservations are processed on a first-come, first-served basis
- The first to reserve will be the first to receive the book
- Your priority number indicates your position (1 means you are next)
- Reservations expire automatically after 30 days
- When the book is available, it will be assigned automatically

## 11. Frequently Asked Questions

### Account and Access

**Q: I forgot my password, what do I do?**
A: Click "Forgot your password" on the login screen. You will receive an email with instructions to reset it.

**Q: Can I change my email address?**
A: Yes, access your profile and update your email address.

### Purchases

**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**
A: We accept cash on delivery and credit/debit cards through Stripe.

**Q: Can I cancel an order?**
A: Contact customer service as soon as possible to request cancellation.

**Q: What is the shipping cost?**
A: Shipping cost is calculated based on your location. Orders over $500 have free shipping.

### Loans

**Q: How long can I keep a borrowed book?**
A: The loan period is 14 days from the request date.

**Q: What happens if I don't return a book on time?**
A: Overdue books may incur late fees. We recommend returning them on time.

**Q: How many books can I have on loan at once?**
A: There is no limit on the number of books you can borrow, as long as they are available.

### Reservations

**Q: How do I know my reservation is active?**
A: You can check the status in "My Reservations". Active reservations show your position in the waiting list.

**Q: How long is a reservation valid?**
A: Reservations expire automatically after 30 days if the book is not available.

**Q: Will I be notified when the book is available?**
A: Yes, the system automatically assigns the book when available and updates your reservation.
